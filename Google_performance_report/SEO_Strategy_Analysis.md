## 3. SEO Enhancements: How to Dominate the Tamil Nadu Market

To turn these impressions into actual lead conversions and trend hard locally, here is what you need to do next:

### 🚀 A. Hyper-Local Content Creation (The "Town/District" Strategy)
You want to trend in Tamil Nadu. The easiest way to get customers is to write hyper-specific location pages. Google loves ranking local businesses for local searches.
*   **Write local guides**: "TNEB Net Metering Process in Cuddalore (2026 Guide)" or "How to Apply for PM Surya Ghar Subsidy in Puducherry."
*   **Case Studies**: Create a new blog taxonomy for "Installations." Post short stories with photos: "Recent 5kW Bifacial Installation in Panruti – See the Savings."

### 🗣️ B. Embrace "Tanglish" and Regional Nuances
Most people in TN will search in English, but they use local terminology.
*   Instead of just "electricity bill," use keywords like **"TNEB bill reduction"**, **"EB bill calculation for solar"**, or **"Current bill savings."**
*   Consider adding an FAQ block at the bottom of pages that translates common solar terms into Tamil (e.g., Panel, Inverter, Grid) to capture long-tail voice searches.

### 💰 C. Turn Traffic into Leads with the Solar Calculator
You are getting clicks for "1kw solar panel unit generation." People want to know the math.
*   **Enhancement**: Your `calc.jsx` page is great, but you should aggressively link to it from inside your blog posts! 
*   In the middle of your "Mono vs Bifacial" post, add a button that says: **"Calculate how many Bifacial panels your house needs →"** and link it to the calculator.

### ⭐ D. Google Business Profile & Local Signals
Search Console is for websites, but for local businesses, Google Maps is the biggest driver of physical sales.
*   Ensure the address and phone number on your Google Business Profile matches the footer (`/components/Footer.jsx`) **exactly**.
*   Embed your Google Maps location directly on your `contact.jsx` page (if you haven't already) so Google's bots explicitly connect your domain to your physical Cuddalore office.
*   **Reviews**: Reply to every single Google Review including keywords. (e.g., "Thanks for choosing Surya's Solar for your 3kW Waaree installation in Neyveli!")
