# 5 Reasons Why Solar Energy is Perfect for Cuddalore Homes

As electricity costs continue to rise and environmental concerns grow, more Cuddalore families are turning to solar energy. Here's why solar power is the perfect solution for homes in our region.

## 1. Abundant Sunshine Year-Round

Cuddalore enjoys **300+ sunny days** per year, making it an ideal location for solar energy generation. Our tropical climate ensures consistent power production throughout the year, maximizing your return on investment.

## 2. Massive Savings on Electricity Bills

Homeowners who switch to solar report savings of **up to 90%** on their electricity bills. With bi-monthly bills often exceeding ₹5,000, solar panels can pay for themselves in just 3-5 years.

### Average Savings Breakdown:
- **Year 1-5**: Recover installation costs
- **Year 6-25**: Pure savings (₹15,000 - ₹30,000 annually)
- **Lifetime**: ₹4-8 lakhs in total savings

## 3. Government Subsidies Available

The Tamil Nadu government offers generous subsidies for residential solar installations:

- Up to **₹18,000 per kW** for systems up to 3 kW
- Simplified application process
- **We handle all paperwork** at Surya's Solar

## 4. Increase Your Property Value

Homes with solar installations sell for **15-20% more** than comparable homes without solar. It's an investment that pays dividends even if you decide to sell.

## 5. Environmental Impact

By going solar, an average Cuddalore home can:
- Offset **4 tons of CO₂** annually
- Equivalent to planting **100 trees** every year
- Contribute to cleaner air for future generations
