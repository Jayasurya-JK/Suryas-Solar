### The Investment (Outflow)

*   **Component Quality:** Tier-1 Bifacial / Mono-PERC Panels + 10 Year Warranty Inverter.
*   **Gross Project Cost:** ₹1,90,000.

### The Opportunity Cost
*   Your current Bi-Monthly [TNEB Bill](/blog/2026-01-17-understanding-tneb-solar-bill) is **₹5,000**.
*   Annual Electricity Expense: **₹30,000**.
*   **Goal:** Reduce this to Zero.

If you invested that same ₹1.12 Lakhs elsewhere, where would you stand after 25 years?

##  Can Anything Go Wrong? (Risk Assessment)

As honest auditors, we must look at the downside. Here is what kills ROI:

1.  **Inverter Failure:** Grid inverters usually last 10-12 years. We have factored in a replacement cost in Year 11.
2.  **Cyclones (The Cuddalore Factor):** This is a wind zone. If your structure breaks in a cyclone, your ROI is zero.
    *   *Mitigation:* This is why we insist on **Hot-Dip Galvanized Structures** and concrete pedestals. It acts as insurance for your investment.
3.  **Dust/Shadow:** If you don't clean it, generation drops 25%.
    *   *Mitigation:* Clean it every 15 days. It costs ₹0 if you do it yourself.
