## What Influences Solar Installation Cost in Tamil Nadu?

Not all solar quotes are the same. If you see a "cheap" quote, it often means corners are being cut. Here is what actually goes into the cost of a premium solar installation in **Cuddalore and Puducherry**.

### 1. Solar Panel Technology
The type of panel you choose significantly impacts the price and performance.
*   **Polycrystalline (Old Tech):** Cheaper but less efficient. We generally do not recommend these for residential rooftops anymore.
*   **Mono PERC Half-Cut (Standard):** The current industry standard. They offer excellent efficiency (around 21%) and work well in low light.
*   **N-Type TOPCon (Premium):** The latest technology with efficiency over 22.5%. They degrade slower over time but cost slightly more.

### 2. Inverter Quality
The inverter is the heart of your system. A cheap inverter may fail in 3-5 years, while a quality one lasts 10-12 years.
*   **We use:** Top-tier brands like **Growatt, Solis, or Enphase** (Microinverters) that come with extended warranties and reliable service centers in Tamil Nadu.

### 3. Mounting Structure (Crucial for Coastal Areas)
This is often overlooked. In coastal areas like **Cuddalore, Puducherry, and Chidambaram**, standard iron structures rust within 2-3 years due to salt air.
*   **Our Standard:** We use **Hot Dip Galvanized (HDG)** structures (80 microns zinc coating) which are rust-proof and can withstand high wind speeds during cyclones.
*   **Cost Impact:** HDG structures cost about 10-15% more than standard GI but double the lifespan of your system.

### 4. Wiring & Safety Components
Safety is non-negotiable.
*   **AC/DC Distribution Boxes (AJB/ACDB):** Protects your home from surges.
*   **Earthing:** We install separate earthing for the inverter, panels, and lightning arrester.
*   **Cables:** UV-protected DC cables are essential to prevent insulation breakdown under the hot Tamil Nadu sun.

## Hidden Costs You Should Know About

When budgeting for solar, keep these potential extra costs in mind so there are no surprises.

*   **Net Metering Charges:** You need to pay TNEB (or PED in Puducherry) for the bi-directional meter and testing fees. This is usually **₹3,000 – ₹5,000**.
*   **Structure Elevation:** If you want the panels raised (to use the roof space underneath for a terrace garden or drying clothes), the steel requirement increases, adding **₹15,000 – ₹25,000** to the cost.
*   **Cable Length:** Standard quotes include ~30 meters of cable. If your inverter is far from the panels or the main distribution board, extra cabling is charged per meter.
*   **Old Wiring Upgrade:** If your home has very old wiring or an outdated fuse box, we may recommend upgrading it to a modern MCB box for safety before connecting the solar system.
